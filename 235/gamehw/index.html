Lethal League Blaze


Publishing Info

  • Published by: Team Reptile
  • Developed by: Team Reptile
  • Initial Release: Oct 24, 2018

Description

Lethal League Blaze is an intense, high speed ball game, with unique characters, outta sight sounds and none of that weak shit.
Whack an anti-gravity ball back and forth with your opponent until one of you gets hit with it, dodgeball style! Every strike of the ball increases its speed, so be careful! This game can be played locally, or online in custom or ranked lobbies.

Game Categories

  • Genre: Action
  • Perspective: Side view
  • Visual: 2D Scrolling
  • Gameplay: Fighting
  • Players: 1-4, local or online
